Ronnie Peterson, "SuperSwede," was an electrifyingly fast driver whose natural talent often overshadowed his tactical approach. His spectacular, flamboyant style made him a fan favourite, frequently seen drifting through corners on the absolute limit. A true speed merchant, Peterson achieved {{driver_poles}} pole positions and {{driver_wins}} Grand Prix victories, predominantly with Lotus. Tragically, he died from complications following a crash at the 1978 Italian Grand Prix, a season in which he was a strong contender for the World Championship.
