Marcus Ericsson competed in {{driver_races}} Formula 1 Grands Prix, primarily for Sauber. Known for his consistent, if not spectacular, driving, he spent four full seasons in the sport. While he never achieved a podium finish, Ericsson was a reliable presence for the midfield teams he represented, often outperforming his machinery. His F1 career concluded after the 2018 season, after which he transitioned to IndyCar, notably winning the Indianapolis 500.
