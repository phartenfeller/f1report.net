Adrian Sutil was a fast, yet at times erratic, midfield runner who spent the majority of his Formula 1 career with Force India. Known for his aggressive driving style and qualifying pace, he often extracted strong performances from his machinery. Despite showing flashes of brilliance, including a memorable fourth-place finish at the 2009 Italian Grand Prix, Sutil never quite reached the podium in his {{driver_races}} starts. His career was marked by solid, if unspectacular, consistency for a team often battling towards the rear of the grid.
