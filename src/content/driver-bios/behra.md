Jean Behra, a fiery and tenacious French driver, competed in {{driver_races}} World Championship Grands Prix. Known for his aggressive driving style and sheer determination, he often extracted the maximum from his machinery, regardless of its capabilities. Behra achieved {{driver_podiums}} podium finishes and {{driver_poles}} pole positions during his F1 career, driving for various teams including Gordini, Maserati, and BRM. His passion for racing extended beyond Formula 1, as he was also a successful sports car and motorcycle racer. Behra's career was tragically cut short in 1959 during a sports car race at the AVUS circuit.
