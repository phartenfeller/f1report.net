Mark Webber was a tenacious and highly respected competitor known for his aggressive yet fair driving style. After making his debut in 2002, he became a consistent front-runner, particularly during his successful tenure with Red Bull Racing. Webber secured {{driver_wins}} Grand Prix victories and {{driver_podiums}} podium finishes, often battling at the sharp end of the grid. Though he never clinched a world championship, his resilience and popular persona made him a beloved figure in the paddock and among fans.
