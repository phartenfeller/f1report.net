René Arnoux was a mercurial talent whose career spanned the late 1970s and 1980s, primarily with Renault and Ferrari. Known for his aggressive, flamboyant driving style and exceptional qualifying pace, he was a formidable competitor, particularly in turbo-era cars. While never quite achieving a World Championship, his {{driver_wins}} victories and memorable duels, most notably with Gilles Villeneuve at Dijon in 1979, cemented his place as one of the era's most exciting figures.
