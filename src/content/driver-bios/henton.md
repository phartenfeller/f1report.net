Desmond "Des" Henton was a British driver whose Formula 1 career was brief but notable for its timing. Having established himself in lower formulae, including a strong showing in Formula 2, Henton made his Grand Prix debut in 1979. He competed in {{driver_races}} races for several privateer teams, including Ensign and Arrows, often struggling with uncompetitive machinery. Despite never scoring a point, his presence during a period of significant technical innovation and burgeoning ground effect aerodynamics adds to the rich tapestry of late 1970s and early 1980s F1.
