The Netherlands has a rich history in Formula 1, predominantly through the passionate support for its drivers. While the nation has produced numerous Grand Prix contenders, the most notable and successful has been Max Verstappen, who embodies the fearless and aggressive driving style often associated with Dutch racers. His rapid ascent and multiple championships have ignited a new era of orange-clad fervor in the sport. Beyond Verstappen, drivers like Jos Verstappen and Jan Lammers also carved out careers, contributing to the country's enduring presence on the grid and its vibrant fan culture.
