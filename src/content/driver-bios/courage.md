Courage, though never an official Formula 1 competitor, represents a foundational characteristic essential to every driver who has ever strapped into a grand prix car. It embodies the bravery required to push the limits of speed and machine, facing down inherent risks for the pursuit of victory. This intangible quality is evident in daring overtakes, steadfast defense, and the unwavering commitment to a sport where milliseconds can define success or failure. It is the spirit that fuels every pole position, every podium finish, and ultimately, every championship title.
