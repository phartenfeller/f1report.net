Gerino Gerini, an Italian privateer, participated in a mere handful of Formula 1 World Championship races in the mid-1950s. Primarily known for his entries in the Italian Grand Prix, his career was typical of many gentleman drivers of the era who competed in Maserati and Ferrari machinery. While he never scored championship points, his presence contributed to the vibrant, if dangerous, atmosphere of early Grand Prix racing.
