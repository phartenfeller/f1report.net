After a dominant career in motorcycle racing, he transitioned to Formula 1, quickly demonstrating his exceptional talent and adaptability. His meticulous approach and deep understanding of vehicle dynamics contributed to his success, culminating in the 1964 Formula 1 World Championship with Ferrari.
