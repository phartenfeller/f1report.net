Piero Sanesi was an Italian racing driver who competed in a single Formula 1 World Championship race. His debut came at the 1950 Italian Grand Prix, driving a privateer Alfa Romeo 158. Sanesi's participation in the inaugural F1 season places him among the sport's earliest competitors, though his career at the top level was brief. Despite not finishing the race, his entry is part of the foundational history of Formula 1.
