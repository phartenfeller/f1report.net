Sébastien Buemi is a Swiss driver who competed in Formula 1 for Toro Rosso from 2009 to 2011, participating in {{driver_races}} Grands Prix. Known for his consistent, if not spectacular, performances, he scored points regularly during his tenure. Following his F1 career, Buemi achieved significant success in other categories, notably winning multiple World Endurance Championships and the 24 Hours of Le Mans, as well as an FIA Formula E title, solidifying his reputation as a versatile and accomplished racing driver.
