Swedish musician and racing driver Slim Borgudd entered Formula 1 at the relatively late age of 34, making his debut with ATS in 1981. Known for his distinctive beard and a sponsorship deal linked to the band ABBA, Borgudd competed in {{driver_races}} Grands Prix. While he never scored points, his presence added a unique, charismatic note to the paddock, marking him as one of F1's more unconventional personalities. After his brief F1 stint, he continued a successful career in truck racing.
