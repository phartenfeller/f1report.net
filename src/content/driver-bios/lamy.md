Christian Lamy, often known as Nicolas Lamy, was a French driver who participated in Formula 1 in 1950. He entered one Grand Prix, the 1950 Monaco Grand Prix, driving a privateer Talbot-Lago. Lamy did not start the race, making his Formula 1 career brief and without competitive laps. His single entry places him among the many privateer entrants in the early years of the championship.
