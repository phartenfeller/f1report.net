Eliseo Salazar's Formula 1 career spanned parts of six seasons, primarily with smaller, independent teams. The Chilean driver became known for his persistent efforts to secure a place on the grid, often bringing his own sponsorship. While he scored points on occasion, his tenure is perhaps best remembered for a notable on-track altercation with Eddie Irvine at the 1997 Argentine Grand Prix after a collision.
