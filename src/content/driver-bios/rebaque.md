Héctor Rebaque was a Mexican privateer who arrived in Formula 1 in 1977, famously campaigning his own Team Rebaque chassis in 1978 after initially running customer Lotuses. Though his independent effort struggled for consistent results, he earned respect for his determination and personal investment in his racing. His persistence paid off when he joined Brabham in 1979 as Nelson Piquet's teammate, securing his best finish of 6th at the 1980 Canadian Grand Prix.
