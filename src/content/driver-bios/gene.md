Marc Gené, a Spanish driver, competed in {{driver_races}} Formula 1 Grands Prix across multiple seasons. While he never achieved a podium finish, his career is notable for his long tenure as a test and reserve driver for Scuderia Ferrari, a role he held for over a decade. His extensive development work behind the scenes contributed significantly to Ferrari's successes during the Schumacher era and beyond, showcasing his technical acumen and consistent feedback. Gené's post-F1 career has also seen him achieve success in sports car racing, including multiple wins at the 24 Hours of Le Mans.
