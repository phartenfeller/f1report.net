Jean-Éric Vergne, a highly-rated junior, raced for Toro Rosso for three seasons, demonstrating flashes of speed and aggressive overtaking. Though he never secured a podium in his {{driver_races}} starts, he consistently outperformed his machinery and often ran close to his more experienced teammate, Daniel Ricciardo. His F1 career ended prematurely in 2014, but Vergne found significant success thereafter in Formula E, where he became a multiple champion, showcasing his adaptability and racecraft.
