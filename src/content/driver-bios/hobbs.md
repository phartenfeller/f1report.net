Jack Doohan, an Australian talent, represents the next generation of motorsport hopefuls, following in the footsteps of his father, motorcycle racing legend Mick Doohan. A consistent front-runner in Formula 2, he has secured multiple victories and shown impressive pace, making him a strong contender for a future Formula 1 seat. His aggressive yet controlled driving style, combined with his dedication to sim racing, highlights a modern approach to driver development. While yet to make his Grand Prix debut, Doohan has participated in several Free Practice sessions with Alpine, demonstrating his potential to join the elite ranks.
