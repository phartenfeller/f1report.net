Toulo de Graffenried was a Swiss nobleman and one of the earliest privateers in the Formula 1 World Championship. His career spanned the formative years of the sport, competing in an era when gentlemen drivers were common. Known for his elegant driving style and sporting demeanor, he often raced his own Maseratis, achieving {{driver_podiums}} podium finishes. De Graffenried's participation helped establish the international character of early Grand Prix racing.
