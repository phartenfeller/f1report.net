Kevin Magnussen, son of former F1 driver Jan Magnussen, made a sensational debut with McLaren in 2014, securing a podium finish in his first race. Known for his aggressive, no-nonsense driving style and tenacious wheel-to-wheel battling, he has carved out a reputation as a fierce competitor on the grid. After stints with McLaren and Renault, he found a long-term home at Haas, becoming a cornerstone of the American outfit and demonstrating remarkable resilience and determination throughout his {{driver_races}} Grand Prix starts.
