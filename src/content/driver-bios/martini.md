Not to be confused with the illustrious Italian vermouth brand, Pierluigi Martini was a tenacious and often spectacular driver from Italy, best known for his long association with the Minardi team. He competed in {{driver_races}} Grands Prix, often punching above the weight of his machinery and delivering memorable qualifying performances. While he never achieved a podium finish, his spirited drives and consistent efforts, particularly at his home Grand Prix, endeared him to fans and cemented his place as a cult hero for the underdog Minardi outfit.
