Francisco Godia Sales, often known simply as 'Godia', was a Spanish privateer who competed in the early years of the Formula 1 World Championship. Driving primarily his own Maserati 250F, he participated in {{driver_races}} Grand Prix races between 1951 and 1958. Godia's career highlight was a notable fourth-place finish at the 1956 German Grand Prix, showcasing his skill against factory teams. His dedication as a private entrant in an era dominated by works teams made him a respected figure in the paddock.
