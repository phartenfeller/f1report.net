Corrado Fabi, brother of accomplished racing driver Teo Fabi, competed in Formula 1 during the early 1980s. Primarily associated with smaller, privateer teams like Osella, Fabi faced an uphill battle against better-funded competitors. Despite the challenges, he demonstrated determination in his {{driver_races}} starts, though podium finishes remained elusive. His career, while brief, is emblematic of the struggles and aspirations of drivers in less competitive machinery during that era.
