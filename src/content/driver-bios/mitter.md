Kurt Ahrens Jr., a German driver, competed in a handful of Formula 1 races, primarily in the late 1960s. Known for his versatility, he also had a successful career in sports car and touring car racing. His Formula 1 appearances, largely with privateer Brabham entries, saw him demonstrate solid, if not spectacular, performances, with a best finish of sixth at the 1969 German Grand Prix. While he never achieved a podium in F1, Ahrens Jr. was a respected figure in German motorsport.
