Derek Warwick, a tenacious and highly-respected British racer, competed in {{driver_races}} Formula 1 Grands Prix, known for his battling spirit and commitment. Despite never achieving a Grand Prix victory, he secured {{driver_podiums}} podium finishes, often in less competitive machinery.
