Allan McNish is a Scottish racing driver best known for his successful career in sports car racing, including three overall victories at the 24 Hours of Le Mans. While his Formula 1 foray was brief, competing in {{driver_races}} Grands Prix, he demonstrated raw speed and commitment. His F1 career with Toyota in 2002 was marked by several strong qualifying performances and an unfortunate heavy crash at Suzuka. Despite not scoring points, McNish's technical expertise and development skills were highly valued by the team.
