Manfred Winkelhock's tragic death at Mosport in 1985 opened a door for fellow German Huub Rothengatter, who took over the Zakspeed seat for the final three races of the season. A journeyman racer, Rothengatter had made his debut in 1984 with Spirit, later driving for Osella and Zakspeed. Across {{driver_races}} starts, he notably finished seventh at the 1985 Canadian Grand Prix. While never scoring a championship point, Rothengatter's perseverance allowed him to compete in Formula 1 for three seasons.
