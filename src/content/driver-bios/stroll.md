Lance Stroll, a Canadian driver, made his Formula 1 debut in 2017 with Williams, becoming the youngest driver to finish on the podium in his rookie season at the Azerbaijan Grand Prix. Known for his strong wet-weather performances and assertive driving style, he has since competed in {{driver_races}} races, securing {{driver_podiums}} podium finishes and {{driver_poles}} pole positions. Stroll's career has seen him transition to Aston Martin (formerly Racing Point), a team co-owned by his father, Lawrence Stroll, where he continues to be a consistent presence on the grid.
