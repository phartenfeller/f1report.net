Dan Gurney was a versatile American racing driver, team owner, and constructor who achieved success across multiple disciplines. A true innovator, he was the first driver to win races in sports cars (FIA World Championship), Formula 1, NASCAR, and IndyCar. Known for his smooth, intelligent driving style and technical acumen, Gurney famously introduced the "Gurney flap" aerodynamic device.
