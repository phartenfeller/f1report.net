Danny Sullivan was an American driver whose brief Formula 1 career in 1983, spanning {{driver_races}} races, saw him compete for the Tyrrell team. Though he didn't score points, his F1 stint was a prelude to a successful career in American open-wheel racing. Sullivan notably won the 1985 Indianapolis 500 with a memorable "spin and win" maneuver, showcasing his car control and resilience. He later achieved the CART championship in 1988, cementing his legacy as a prominent figure in North American motorsport.
