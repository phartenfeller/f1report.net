Shinji Nakano was a Japanese driver who competed in Formula 1 for two seasons. Known for his smooth driving style, he debuted with Prost Grand Prix in 1997, partnering Olivier Panis. Despite often racing with less competitive machinery, Nakano was a consistent finisher, completing 10 of 17 races in his rookie year.
