Narain Karthikeyan holds the distinction of being India's first Formula 1 driver, making his debut in 2005. Primarily associated with the Jordan and HRT teams, his F1 career spanned {{driver_races}} starts across two distinct periods. While competitive machinery often eluded him, Karthikeyan's presence on the grid marked a significant milestone for motorsport in his home country. His best result was a fourth-place finish at the 2005 United States Grand Prix, albeit in a race heavily impacted by tire issues for much of the field.
