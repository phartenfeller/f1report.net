Gijs van Lennep, a Dutch sports car ace, made occasional forays into Formula 1 during the 1970s. Primarily known for his two victories at the 24 Hours of Le Mans, his F1 career was brief but showcased his natural talent. Despite competing in only {{driver_races}} Grands Prix, he managed to score a championship point, demonstrating his ability in a diverse range of machinery. His F1 appearances were often with smaller, privateer teams, highlighting his versatility and determination to compete at the highest level.
