Ivan Capelli, an Italian driver, competed in Formula 1 from 1986 to 1993, known for his smooth style and spirited drives. He achieved {{driver_podiums}} podium finishes over {{driver_races}} starts, most notably with the Leyton House team. His memorable performance at the 1990 French Grand Prix, where he led for much of the race in an unfancied car, showcased his talent and tenacity, nearly securing a shock victory. Though he never won a Grand Prix, Capelli was a respected competitor who consistently extracted strong performances from his machinery.
