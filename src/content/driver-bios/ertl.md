Ertl competed in a single Formula 1 Grand Prix, the 1970 German Grand Prix at Hockenheim. Driving a privateer Lotus 72, he failed to qualify for the event, preventing him from ever starting a championship race. Despite this brief appearance, Ertl's name is remembered as one of the many privateers who attempted to break into the elite world of Formula 1 during a competitive era. His entry highlights the challenges faced by independent drivers against factory-backed teams.
