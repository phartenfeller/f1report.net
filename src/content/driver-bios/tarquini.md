Gabriele Tarquini, often characterized by his distinctive red helmet, was a tenacious and experienced competitor who participated in {{driver_races}} Formula 1 Grands Prix. Despite not achieving a podium finish, his longevity and resilience were notable, driving for several privateer teams such as AGS and Fondmetal during a challenging era for smaller outfits. Tarquini's career was marked by consistent effort and a never-give-up attitude, even as he often found himself battling at the back of the grid. His post-F1 career saw significant success in touring car racing, where he became a multiple world champion.
