Nico Rosberg, son of 1982 World Champion Keke Rosberg, carved his own formidable legacy in Formula 1. Competing in {{driver_races}} races, he demonstrated a cerebral and meticulous approach to racing, often excelling in qualifying to secure {{driver_poles}} pole positions. His career culminated in a fierce rivalry with Mercedes teammate Lewis Hamilton, which he ultimately won to claim the 2016 World Championship before immediately announcing his retirement. Rosberg finished his career with {{driver_wins}} wins and {{driver_podiums}} podiums, becoming one of only two sons of a World Champion to also win the title.
