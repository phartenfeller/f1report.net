Emanuele Pirro, a versatile Italian racer, competed in {{driver_races}} Formula 1 Grands Prix, primarily for Benetton and Dallara. While he didn't score points in F1, his career transcended the sport, achieving significant success in sports car racing. Pirro became a five-time winner of the 24 Hours of Le Mans with Audi, cementing his legacy as an endurance racing legend. His adaptability and consistent performance across different disciplines highlighted his considerable talent beyond the confines of Formula 1.
