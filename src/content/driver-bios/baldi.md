Mauro Baldi was an Italian driver whose Formula 1 career spanned parts of the early to mid-1980s. Known for his aggressive driving style, he competed in {{driver_races}} Grands Prix for Spirit and Arrows, often in challenging machinery. While he never reached the podium in F1, Baldi later found significant success in sports car racing, including an overall victory at the 24 Hours of Le Mans in 1994, solidifying his reputation as a versatile and accomplished endurance racer. His F1 tenure, though brief, showcased his determination against formidable opposition.
