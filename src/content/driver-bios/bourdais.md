Sébastien Bourdais, a four-time Champ Car champion, made a late entry into Formula 1 with Scuderia Toro Rosso in 2008. His F1 career, though brief, showcased flashes of the speed and talent that had dominated American open-wheel racing. Despite a challenging car, he scored points in his debut race at the 2008 Australian Grand Prix, demonstrating his raw pace. He competed in {{driver_races}} Grands Prix, ultimately returning to sports car and open-wheel racing in North America where he continued to achieve significant success.
