Pastor Maldonado competed in {{driver_races}} Formula 1 races, earning a reputation for his aggressive, often spectacular driving style. His career highlight was an unexpected victory at the 2012 Spanish Grand Prix, marking his sole win and only podium finish. While undoubtedly fast on occasion, Maldonado was also prone to crashes, leading to a high-profile, if sometimes controversial, tenure in the sport. He raced for Williams and Lotus, leaving an indelible mark on an era known for its unpredictable midfield battles.
